Forewords for Pilvilinna

Petri Ojala, Apr 5, 2021, Forewords Foreword

We’re moving!

This summer we will be relocating to a new apartment, apply called Pilvilinna, Cloud Castle. The place is really called a cloud castle and it’s in a 100+ year old mental institute neighbourhood.
It will also be our first time living in the “countryside”, outside of Helsinki postal code, about 40 minutes drive to Helsinki central railway station (now 15 minutes walk).

Why blog? Well, there’s quite a bit of things to plan or think about. If one blogs about them, one doesn’t need to google the same things half a dozen times again and again. Plus everything will hopefully get documented as part of the process.
The topics start a bit technical, from networking to home automation, from electric car plans to thoughts about upgrading the cofffee machine but also furniture and art on the walls as well – anything really!

For technical bits the plan is to see how the existing setup will fit in, what should be upgraded and how, and what is worth just to abandon. And let’s not forget about the cloud – it’s cloud castle after all!

Currently the home is running on top of Kubernetes with FreeNAS, Home Assistant, Node Red, Zigbee, MQTT, Philips Hue, Mikrotik switches, EdgeRouter, TP-Link wireless, 802.11ac WiFi, Google Home, Amazon Alexa, Apple TV, Samsung’s 65” LCD TV, and most appliances without connectivity.

I’ll start with reflections to the current setup but eventually we’ll hopefully turn into a home that can be rebuilt with a single Terraform run :)
